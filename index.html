Bacopia


It is a 2-player action game that we made as a five person team in Global Game Jam '14. The main goal is to find your character and blow up your enemy before he/she finds you. Tricky part is that you and your enemy looks all the same. You must guess from the movements of the characters who is the enemy and who are you.

Substance Unknown
